About Us

Nirvana East Indian Restaurant and Lounge is now opened serving you Calgary's finest ethnic food. Nirvanas Banquet facility has been Calgary's most popular venue for birthday parties, corporate meetings, weddings, receptions and many other special occasions which opened its doors in September 2006.

 

Take your Senses to the newest Exclusive East Indian Restaurant in Calgary in the N.E. neighborhood. Accommodating large groups of up to 410 people with its lavishing two floors. Nirvana can satisfy the most elaborate affair. With its antique decor, luxurious space, peaceful ambience can cater to cozy romantic dinners, memorable wedding receptions, corporate meetings or functions.

 

Nirvana is a two level restaurant with a banquet hall and cocktail lounge which specializes in East Indian food and Tandoori grilled sizzlers which are one of a kind. The restaurants food menu is the creation of our Executive Chef Shri Ram Parsad Bihari Lal who brings 35 years of experience from Mumbai's finest dining known as khana khazana in India. The menu features many original recipes, our bar menu is a worldwide selection of cocktails, wines, beers and champagnes.
